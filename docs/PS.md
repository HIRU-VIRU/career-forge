🎉 AI ASCEND Hackathon 2026 – Grand Finale

We are happy to share that our college has been shortlisted to participate in the Finale of AI ASCEND Hackathon 2026! 🚀

Please note the following important details:

📝 Deadline
Final registration form must be submitted before 4:00 PM on 3rd March 2026.

📂 Problem Statement Themes
Teams will build solutions on AWS Academy Learner Labs under these themes:

Governance & Public Services – citizen chatbots, grievance analytics, scheme eligibility engines.

Health & Wellbeing – disease prediction, AI symptom checker, medical image classification.

Education & Employability – personalized learning agents, skill gap analytics, placement matching engines.

Sustainability & Secure Digital India – energy/water optimization, smart waste classification, fraud/phishing detection.

📌 Rules
Each college can nominate maximum 3 teams (one solution per team).

Problem statements must be validated before development.

Solutions must be uploaded in the defined template, saved as Team Name – University Name.

Each team will receive $50 AWS credits, usable only on 7th & 8th March 2026.
